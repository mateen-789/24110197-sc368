KL Rahul: Stylish Batsman

KL Rahul, full name Kannur Lokesh Rahul, is a stylish and versatile Indian cricketer known for his adaptability across all formats. Born on April 18, 1992, in Mangalore, Karnataka, he is a technically sound batsman and an occasional wicketkeeper. Rahul holds the distinction of being one of the few Indian players to score centuries in all three formats of the game. His elegant stroke play and ability to anchor innings make him a key asset for the Indian team. Additionally, Rahul has proven his leadership skills, captaining India in several matches and leading the Lucknow Super Giants in the IPL.
